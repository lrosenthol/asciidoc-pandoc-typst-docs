== Production Guidance

The current layout is intended for white papers, strategy papers, formal reports, and other narrative documents that may eventually grow into multi-section publications.

[source,make]
----
make pdf
make DOC=white-paper typst
----

Recommended working practices:

- keep the main document as an include-driven entrypoint
- store reusable images under `assets/images`
- keep conversion logic in `styles/filters`
- keep visual rules in `styles/typst`
- add new report entrypoints under `manuscript/`

[CAUTION]
====
If you introduce raw backend-specific markup, extension-generated content, or very complex tables, test the conversion early instead of assuming they will survive unchanged.
====
